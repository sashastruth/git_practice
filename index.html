What I Do

After studying textiles at Arts University Bournemouth, I have continued to love all things textiles, embroidery and print based! I predominantly specialise in stitch work and sell small decorative work.

The Process

My primary technique is free machine embroidery, using a circular sewing machine foot and by dropping the teeth on your machine - and then you just stitch as if you;re drawing! Sometimes I work back into my free-machine embroideries with hand stitch and embellishments.
